1. What services are included when I Signup with Phonetrade?

2. How can I POST more then one ITEM?

If you are willing to POST more then one Items, you can select the option on the  bottom of the Posting Form stating as “I want to POST more ITEMS” and Press  “POST REQUEST” button, this will start gathering your previous posts on the  TOP of the Post Offer Form and you can post as much as ITEMS as you want,  our INTELLIGENT POST FUNCTION will start gathering your postings until you  press “POST TO TRADING FLOOR” button. That's all… and now you can see your  posting in a GROUPED form on Trading Floor.
